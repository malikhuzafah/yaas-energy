About Us.

YAAS Group of Engineers is a specialist solar company based in Pakistan. With an extensive experience, we're installing and maintaining solar systems in Residential, commercial, Industrial as well as in Agricultural fields. We pride ourselves on offering quality services and pay keen attention to details. By choosing YAAS as your energy supplier, you can enjoy price security when you lock in long term saving.
